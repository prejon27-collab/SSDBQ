The White Man's Burden

Take up the White Man’s burden— Send forth the best ye breed— Go send your sons to exile To serve your captives' need...

‘The White Man’s Burden,’ is a lie they tell While turning peaceful lands to hell. A man dressed from head to toe in heavy silk.

Take up the White Man's burden-- The savage wars of peace-- Fill full the mouth of Famine And bid the sickness cease;

He counts dividends and carves the land, He gifts a textbook to a mind they’ve already confined To heal the sickness only he had designed.
